# Third-Party Notices

Magent links to and imports metadata from external sources. Those sources keep
their own licenses, terms, and attribution requirements.

Project policy:

- prefer links, source IDs, and concise original summaries over copied text
- do not copy large problem statements, survey text, encyclopedia text, PDFs, or
  datasets unless the license is understood
- keep source URLs and access dates where possible
- treat imported metadata as source-backed catalog data, not Magent-owned
  research content

Known source registries and import notes live under `catalog/` and `docs/`.
